    Description

    The Ride-Hailing API is a backend service that allows users to book and pay for rides using a mobile app.


    This API was built using

  • NodeJS
  • Express.js
  • MongoDB
  • Socket.io for realtime location updates and inapp phone calls
    • Key features
    1. Ride Booking: Users can easily book rides through the app, specifying their pick-up and drop-off locations, and the type of ride they need.
    2. Driver Matching: The app uses location tracking to find nearby drivers and match them with riders who need a ride.
    3. Real-time Location Tracking: The app tracks the real-time location of the rider and driver during the ride, allowing users to see the exact location of their driver and the estimated time of arrival.
    4. In-app Payments: The app allows users to pay for their rides directly through the app, using a variety of payment methods.
    5. Ratings and Reviews: Users can rate their drivers and leave reviews, helping to maintain high-quality service and ensuring the safety of all users.
    6. Customer Support: The app provides a variety of customer support options, such as in-app chat or phone support, to address any issues or concerns users may have.
    7. Driver Management: The app provides a dashboard for drivers to manage their rides and earnings, as well as access to features like navigation and turn-by-turn directions.
    8. Trip History: The app allows users to view their ride history and receipts, providing an easy way to keep track of expenses and past trips.
